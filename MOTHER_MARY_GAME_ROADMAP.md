# Mother Mary Match-3 — Future Roadmap

_Vision doc for enhancing the Marian matching game. Not yet built — the items below are
planned. See STATUS.md for what ships in the current prototype._

## Guiding vision
A **peaceful, prayerful matching experience inspired by the Blessed Mother** — closer to a
pious "Marian garden" or prayer-themed game than a cartoonish candy game. **Beauty and
reverence first.** Market it as a *"peaceful, prayerful matching experience inspired by the
Blessed Mother."* Avoid any cartoonish or mocking tone.

### Why this is acceptable to (99% of) Catholics
- The symbols are **established devotional imagery** used for centuries in art, medals, statues,
  and gardens — flowers, rosaries, candles are commonly offered at Marian shrines.
- We match **blessed objects and natural/heavenly emblems**, never a depiction of Mary herself
  (or any sacred person) — nothing sacred is ever "crushed."
- Wholesome religious games already exist (rosary apps, saint-matching games). The key is
  **beauty + reverence**.

## Current state (v1 — shipping now)
- 3-tier match-3 on the shared 6×7 board engine, Marian symbols (🌹 rose, 👑 crown, ⭐ star,
  🕊️ dove, 💙 heart, ⚜️ fleur-de-lis), tier goals, gentle "fall from heaven" + cascade.
- Tiers themed as a devotional progression; reward scales with tiers cleared.

## Planned enhancements

### Design principles (reverence + beauty)
- **Palette:** traditional Marian **blue, white, gold, and soft pastels**.
- **Icons** feel like *blessed objects or natural symbols* associated with Mary — flowers,
  prayer items, heavenly emblems — never depictions of sacred persons.
- **Animations:** gentle **sparkling light, soft glows, falling rose petals** instead of
  explosive/violent "crushes."

### Level themes (map to / expand the 3 tiers)
- **Fatima**, **Lourdes**, **Guadalupe** — each an apparition-themed level with its own
  background, palette accent, and symbol emphasis.

### Special elements / boosters
- **Rainbow Rosary Chain** — a connector / line-clearer piece (drag along a chain of beads to
  clear a row/column).
- **Limited-time / event backdrops** — **Lourdes Grotto waterfall**, **Guadalupe tilma rose**
  as backgrounds or timed events.
- **"Hail Mary" combo text** — appears on big matches, paired with a **gentle choir or soft
  bells**.
- **"Intercession" booster** — invokes Our Lady's help to clear a section of the board.

### Sound
- Soft bells, gentle choir swells, chime-on-match — nothing jarring; keep the prayerful tone.

### Title ideas (for the standalone/marketed version)
- **Marian Match**, **Rosary Crush**, **Blessed Blooms**, **Immaculate Garden**.

## Open follow-ups
- Commission/produce reverent **icon art** (SVG or raster) to replace emoji placeholders.
- Decide whether this becomes a **standalone mode** or stays an in-campaign round break.
- Playtest tone with a few Catholic reviewers before any public release.
